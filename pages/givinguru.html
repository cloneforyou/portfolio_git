Concept
  • The goal of GivinGuru app is to create a new online gifting experience by enabling a new communication channel between a gift buyer and the recipient
  • The app was design as a “wish list”, the user can create a collection of interesting gifts and let the recipient select the one they wish to get
  • Usability and features Adding a new gift to the list is possible from any ecommerce app and web site
  • The gift recipient gets the wish list and a personal video greeting as well.
