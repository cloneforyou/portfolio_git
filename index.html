About me

I was born in Israel in 1983, and have lived in Tel Aviv for the last eight years. Since I can remember, I have been fascinated by nature and animals especially. In 2007 I finished my military service in the IAF after seven years. During that period of time I was exposed to cutting-edge technology and experienced great challenges. At 2009 I started Industrial design studies at Bezalel Academy of Art and Design in Jerusalem. During that time I also studied at Pratt institute in New York as a part of a student exchange program. Graduated Bezalel Academy of Arts and Design in 2013.
